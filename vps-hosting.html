FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our VPS hosting platform.

What is VPS Hosting?
+
VPS (Virtual Private Server) hosting gives you a dedicated portion of a physical server with guaranteed CPU, RAM, and storage resources. Unlike shared hosting, your resources are not shared with other users — you get a private, isolated environment with full root access and the ability to install any software you need.
What is the difference between managed and unmanaged VPS?
+
An unmanaged VPS gives you full root access and complete control — you handle all server administration, updates, and security. A managed VPS includes our support team handling server setup, security patches, monitoring, and troubleshooting. We offer Managed Support as an add-on for $15/month, ideal for businesses without dedicated DevOps staff.
Can I upgrade my plan at any time?
+
Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade your VPS resources (CPU, RAM, storage) at any time from your control panel. Upgrades are applied with minimal downtime (usually a brief reboot). We pro-rate any billing differences so you only pay for what you use. Downgrading is also available with advance notice.
Do you provide full root access?
+
Yes. Every GENCORE IT VPS plan comes with complete root (Linux) or Administrator (Windows) access. You have full control over your server, including the ability to install any software, modify system configurations, set up firewalls, and customize the entire environment to your needs.
Which operating systems are supported?
+
We support a wide range of operating systems including Ubuntu 20.04/22.04/24.04, Debian 11/12, AlmaLinux 8/9, Rocky Linux 8/9, CentOS Stream, Fedora, and Windows Server 2019/2022. We also offer pre-configured templates for Docker, cPanel, and custom OS installations via ISO upload on request.
Is DDoS protection included with all plans?
+
Basic DDoS mitigation (up to 10 Gbps) is included free with all VPS plans at the network level. For enhanced protection up to 500 Gbps with advanced filtering and analytics, you can add our DDoS Protection add-on for $7/month. This is recommended for public-facing applications and gaming servers.
How fast is server deployment?
+
Our automated provisioning system deploys new VPS servers in under 60 seconds. Once payment is confirmed, you'll receive SSH credentials, server IP, and control panel access almost immediately. There are no manual steps or waiting periods. You can literally go from order to running a live application in under 5 minutes.
Are backups included with VPS plans?
+
Weekly automated backups are included in all plans at no extra cost. For daily backups with 30-day retention and one-click restore, you can add our Backup add-on for $5/month. All backups are stored in a geographically separate location from your VPS for maximum data safety. You can also create manual snapshots at any time.
